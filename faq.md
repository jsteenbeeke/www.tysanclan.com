    <h1>Frequently Asked Questions</h1>
    <p>Everything you wanted to know about The Tysan Clan</p>
</div>

<div class="grid-2">
    <div class="card">
        <h3>🏛️ Who started all this?</h3>
        <p>We don't know his real name, though his Battle.net alias was <strong>(TYC)Blayde</strong>, and he allegedly created Tysan in December 1997. None of our existing members have ever met him, and he has become something of a mythical person.</p>
        <p>A far less mythical founding figure was <strong>Tadrith</strong>, who some of our existing members have actually gamed with.</p>
        <p>Tysan in its current form is the result of years of evolution and reorganization.</p>
    </div>
    
    <div class="card">
        <h3>📅 Were you really founded in 1997?</h3>
        <p>The honest answer is we're not entirely sure, though we do use December 1997 as the basis for anniversaries. Our site has mentioned 1997 as our founding year since at least 2000, though it also mentioned 1999 as the date Tysan was "refounded".</p>
        <p>We have confirmed that Tysan was active in 1999 through old posts by members on the Battle.net Clan Hall forum, and those members were still there when <strong>Prospero</strong> joined in 2001 (and he's still here).</p>
        <p>We've had two people visit our forums who claimed to have been part of Tysan in 1997 or 1998. On the other hand, our friends from the Defenders of Camelot (who are definitely older than us) are adamant that Tysan did not exist prior to 1999.</p>
        <p><strong>Either way, we've been here over twenty years.</strong></p>
    </div>
    
    <div class="card">
        <h3>🎮 How do I join?</h3>
        <p>Head on over to our <a href="https://discord.gg/wMyAfp7" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Discord server</a> and ask us. We don't really have a formal process these days, so please give us an excuse to figure out a process for this.</p>
        <p>We're a pretty laid-back community at this point—just come hang out, game with us, and if it feels like a good fit, we'll make it official.</p>
    </div>
    
    <div class="card">
        <h3>👑 Can I get my old rank back?</h3>
        <p><strong>No.</strong> Besides, we more or less abolished our ranking system a few years ago.</p>
        <p><strong>LordInfiniti</strong> is still our Truthsayer, and <strong>Prospero</strong> hosts the website and runs the Discord server and Minecraft server. Beyond that, we're all just members and friends.</p>
    </div>
    
    <div class="card">
        <h3>🎨 Who designed this site?</h3>
        <p>Originally <strong>Prospero</strong>, though he's not a web designer and one of his mottos is "for the love of God don't let me design user interfaces".</p>
        <p>After he got bored by the Minecraft design he had <strong>GitHub Copilot</strong> vibe-code a different design in January 2026.</p>
    </div>
    
    <div class="card">
        <h3>💬 What games do you play?</h3>
        <p>We started with Blizzard games (Starcraft, Diablo, Warcraft) and spent many years in World of Warcraft. These days we play whatever catches our interest—from indie co-op games to the latest multiplayer releases.</p>
        <p>We also run a Minecraft server for members who enjoy that. Check out our <a href="/minecraft">Minecraft page</a> for details.</p>
    </div>
    
    <div class="card">
        <h3>🌍 Where are you located?</h3>
        <p>Our members are scattered across different countries, though many of us are based in Europe and North America. We primarily communicate in English on our Discord server.</p>
    </div>
    
    <div class="card">
        <h3>📜 Do you have rules?</h3>
        <p>Yes! Check out our <a href="/regulations">Regulations</a> page for our community guidelines. They're pretty straightforward—basically, don't be a jerk, respect other members, and uphold the values that have kept us together for decades.</p>
        <p>We also have our formal <a href="/charter">Charter</a> if you want to read the full governance structure (spoiler: we're pretty casual about it these days).</p>
    </div>

## What happened to your old site?

It was archived in favor of this one. We were barely using the old site, and could no longer justify the expenses of keeping it up. Prospero does still have a project hidden somewhere for a more interactive and modern site but he rarely works on it, and frankly there's no need for it currently.
