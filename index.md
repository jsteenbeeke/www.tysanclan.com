## Welcome Home

The Tysan Clan is a gaming community that was originally founded in 1997, and has been active for most of that time. 
As most of our members have left their teenage years behind them and now have many adult responsibilities, we are not
quite as active as we used to be.

We still game together from time to time, but no longer on a daily basis. What remains constant is the friendship, 
the banter, and the shared history that spans more than a quarter century.

## Get in Touch

Want to say hello? Head over to our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/wMyAfp7). Doesn't matter if you're an old member, 
an old friend, or someone new who wants to get to know us—you're welcome to stop by and chat.

We're always happy to reminisce about the old days, discuss the latest games, or just hang out in voice chat while 
playing whatever catches our fancy.
